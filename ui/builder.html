PM & Tech Rehaul — Balance UI

Manufacturing industry PMs
Break-even ladder — FULL break-even: output price (% of base) at which output revenue = input goods + wages, inputs at base. Lower = survives cheaper markets. Shaded = each tier's target range.
Scenario panel — buy/sell orders & prices — building inputs = BUY orders, outputs = SELL orders (each building's goods × its Number). Add extra buy/sell to model pops & trade. The price column's toggle switches between locked (edit each % by hand) and auto (recomputed live from orders: price = base × [1 + 0.75·clamp((buy−sell)/min(buy,sell), ±1)], propagated to every building). Click a section header to fold it. Hover an industry below to flag its goods here.
Workforce panel — base wage — wages now come from each building's workforce × the base wage per profession (weight = vanilla wage_weight, non-discriminated). Changing it recomputes every building's BE / profit.
Build history — snapshots of settings; stored in this browser. Restore to roll back.
